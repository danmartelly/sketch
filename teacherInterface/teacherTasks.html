Sunday Driver

Jerry goes out for a 30 minute drive. Unfortunately, there's something wrong with his car and it can only accelerate at a constant rate of 1km/hour/minute. His brake time is not affected and is not necessarily constant.

This is how Jerry's drive goes

  1. Jerry starts at rest.
  2. For the first 5 minutes, Jerry accelerates. After that his acceleration drops to 0.
  3. At minute 15, he sees a stop sign and begins applying the brakes.
  4. At minute 16, his car has stopped.
  5. At minute 17, he accelerates for the next 3 minutes.
  6. At minute 28, he's close to his destination and slowly applies his brakes until the car stops at minute 30

Sketch the velocity of the car over the 30 minute period.
